Health Benefits of Peanuts

Bigger Feet, Smaller (More Nimble) Fingers, Consistent Toe Size

By Jack Loughran

Bigger Feet

Studies have shown that eating more peanuts gives you bigger feet. Here are some reasons why you might want bigger feet:

  • Easier to find shoe size
  • Girls notice big feet
  • Excuse to buy new shoes
  • Your current socks are too lose

Smaller (More Nimble) Fingers

Everyone knows that peanuts make your fingers smaller (and more nimble). What you might not know, is that peanuts are actually legumes and not nuts! Other legumes:

  • alfalfa
  • clover
  • peas
  • beans
  • lentils
  • lupin bean
  • mesquite
  • carob
  • soybeans
  • tamarind

Consistent Toe Size

Everyone wants a more consistent toe size. Not everyone realizes that a diet rich in peanuts can help keep your toe size up to 10% more consistent (Source). Other foods that contain Vitamin JSRC (for toe size consistancy) are yams, carrots, and Oreos.
